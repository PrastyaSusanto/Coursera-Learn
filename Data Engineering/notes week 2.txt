DATA ENGINEERING DEFINED



Generate: create data from source
Ingestion:
stream: contiuously almost real time, available shortly after it is produced

batch: ingest data in batch processes, once every day,etc
based on predetermined time interval
or preset size threshold

transformation:
Query etc
Involve data modelling: choosing a coherent structure
for your data to make it useful for business 

Serving:
Serve data for dashboard, ML, etc




DataOps:
improves the development processes
Pilar of DevOps:
Automation, Observabily and monitoring, Incident Response


Orchestration:
System to make sure process is handled successfully through schedulling,
automation, etc
