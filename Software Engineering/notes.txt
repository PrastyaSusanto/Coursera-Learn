Software Engineering (SE) vs Software Developer (SD):
SE build system, SD specific function
SE: Using programming languages and engineering principles
to build products




Software Development Life Cycle (SDLC):
Cycle of planning, design, and implementation 
of Software
Intially used the waterfall method


Advantages of the SDLC
a. Improves efficiency and reduce risks
b. Team members know what they should be working on and when
c. Respond to changing requirements
d. Solve problems early in the process
e. Reduces overlapping responsibilities


SDLC
1. Planning
2. Design
3. Development
4. Testing
5. Deployment
